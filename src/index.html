  • Inspiring people to care about our planet

    Part of the photo exhibition “Take care – inspiring people to care about our planet,” that the U.S. Embassy in Slovenia prepared together with National Geographic Slovenia, will be on display in Triglav National Park from March 6 to June 9, 2013.   Photos and more information  »

  • SC Velenje Hosts Embassy and Washington, DC Visitors

    School Center Velenje hosted Deputy Assistant Secretary of State in the Bureau of European and Eurasian Affairs, Bay Fang, and Public Affairs Officer Christopher Wurst. 

  • A warm (and delicious) melding of Hungary and Slovenia at Lendava

    A new U.S. Embassy music project called "Project R.E.M.: Pure Energy Music" was released on September 8. The project featured 16 songs by popular Slovenian artists covering tracks ranging from the early 1980’s right up to material from 2011.
